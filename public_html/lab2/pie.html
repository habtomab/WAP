Grandma's Lemon Merinque Pie

One 9 inch pie
30 Min-Prep Time
10 Min-Cook Time
40 Min-Total
8 Servings

INGREDINTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarsh
  • 1/4 tps salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 leamons ,juiced and zested
  • 2 1/4 tps butter
  • 4 egg yolks,beaten
  • 1(9 inch) pie crust,baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 1/4 tps white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven:Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).
  2. Make Lemon Feeling:In medium sausepan...
    • Whisk together one cup sugar,flour,cornstarch,and salt.
    • Stir in water,lemon juice,and lemon zest.
    • Cook over medium-high heat,strring frequently,unitl mixture comes to a boil.
    • Sire in butter.
    • Place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradualy whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
    • Whisk egg yolks mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
    • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while string constantly until thick.
    • Remove from heat.
    • Pour filling into baked patry shell.
  3. Make meringue:In a large glass or metal bowl...
    • Whipe egg whites until foamy
    • Add sugar gradually ,and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
    • Spread meringue over pie,sealing the edges at the crust.
  4. Bake:Bake preheated oven for 10 minutes,or until meringue is golden brown.
